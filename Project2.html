Naomittal Form - Self-Hosted Form Submission API

ROLE:

Freelance Full-Stack Developer, Systems Administrator

TOOLS:

Node.js, Express, JavaScript (ES6+), HTML5, CSS3, SQLite, Linux (Ubuntu), NGINX, Cloudflare (DNS/SSL), VS Code

Project Overview

A secure, cost-saving alternative to third-party form processors, built to handle customer inquiries directly on my own infrastructure.

Key Highlights:
  • Stack: Node.js, Express, SQLite, Cloudflare
  • Developed a lightweight backend to receive, validate, and store contact/booking form data.
  • Implemented spam filtering and secure data handling to protect customer information.
  • Configured email notifications for immediate follow-up.
  • Integrated seamlessly into the Michelle Nails & Spa website, replacing paid form services.
  • Reduced monthly costs while maintaining full control over data ownership and uptime.


Outcome:
  • Delivered faster, more reliable form submissions while eliminating SaaS fees and keeping all customer data in-house.
