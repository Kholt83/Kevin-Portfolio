About Me

My journey into the world of mechanics and manufacturing began at the age of five, inspired by my father—a Volkswagen mechanic and also my hero. Growing up with a passion for fixing and understanding how things ticked, My early hands-on experiences set the stage for a career grounded in practical knowledge and relentless curiosity.

My professional career started in boat manufacturing, where I honed my skills crafting high-quality Ski & Wakeboard boats. From the shop floor, I progressed to repairing tools for the night shift, a role that combined my technical aptitude with problem-solving instincts. This hands-on work laid the foundation for my transition into maintenance—a pivotal move that expanded my responsibilities and became the springboard for my leadership development and project oversight capabilities.

My leadership style is rooted in collaboration and mutual respect. As a round-table leader, I value every team member’s input and foster an environment where debate and learning thrive. I believe leadership is about riding alongside your team, sharing both victories and challenges, and never abandoning the people who depend on you.

Over the years, I have evolved from a skilled maintenance professional into a hybrid powerhouse—merging my industrial expertise with emerging technology. Recognizing the shifting industry landscape, I'm actively pivoting towards the tech sector, leveraging my background to bridge the gap between hands-on work and digital innovation.

Though I don't come from a traditional educational background, my intelligence, adaptability, and dedication to continuous learning have propelled me forward. I encourage others with similar roots to embrace new skills and stay relevant in a rapidly changing world.

Today, I stand as a versatile professional, ready to lead in roles that combine the grit of maintenance with the precision of technology—a true example of adaptability and growth in the modern workforce.
